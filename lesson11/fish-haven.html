Light the Tree

by Bob Gallagher

Christmas. The lights. The smell of gingerbread. Kids running around playing with the snow. It’s the best time of year! Come join us to light the city town’s Christmas tree this year. On December 12, we will gather in front of the town hall and celebrate together. We will have food and drinks available for all of you. If you want, bring your dessert to share with others.

During the celebration, we would like to invite to participate in the charity potluck and be generous with your offerings. All the money will be sent to help children have a better Christmas in Africa. We do this every year, and it is touching how generous people are. Let’s help as many people as we can to have a merry Christmas.
