Freedom Days

by Tom Hollins

I was recently reflecting upon the meaning of the "Freedom Days" celebration. I was reflecting on those dark days when freedom was not part of our culture. For many years, our forefathers had to battle against slavery and monarchy subjugation. England was pressing upon the colonies to get more tax money. Men were driven from their home countries to be exploited. Sometimes, we take freedom for granted.

Preston celebrates the joy of freedom by gathering in the city center and sharing food, playing sports, and rehearsing different historical happenings each night. What a great activity for you and your family! If you love freedom, as we do, do not miss the special “Freedom Potluck on Tuesday night.

Let’s enjoy together these three days of celebration! And don’t forget, Preston is the home of the most delicious funeral potatoes in the world!
