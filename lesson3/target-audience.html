Target Audience

The target audience for the website is described below:

  • Who: General population of the area interested in knowing the weather forecast. Outdoor enthusiasts who want to plan activities such as camping, hiking, rock climbing, etc. People who are interested in knowing which events will be held in the areas of interest.
  • Age: 10 to 100
  • Education: Visitors of the website will come from different educational backgrounds. People with high school diplomas, university degrees, master's, or PhD's may use our service to plan their activities of just find out the weather for the day.
  • Income: Visitors of the website may come from different income backgrounds. The majority of the people will earn between $30,000 to $75,000.
  • Motivation: The motivation visit the website it to have easy access to all the information related to weather, outdoor activities, and events happening in the area, all in one place.
  • Family Status: Visitors will come from a different family background, ranging from single individuals, families, and groups of friends.
  • Technology: Seeing the rise of mobile devices, it is expected that the majority of the customers will access their website from a mobile device. Few people will use the desktop version.
