Site Purpose

As the name indicates, the primary purpose of the website is to find out what the weather will be like today.
A secondary purpose is to see which activites will be recommended for today. It will list events, hiking trails, and any outdoor activity for the day of choice.
As a third purpose, it will also forecast the weather a week in advance so the client can plan an activity further in the future.
